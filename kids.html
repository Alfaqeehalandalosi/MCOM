6-weeks—Preschool

infants to preschool
From the babies to the 5 year-olds, our desire is that your preschoolers experience the presence of God. We want your preschoolers to learn three key things while they’re with us: God made them; God loves them; Jesus wants to be their friend forever! Those three things will be reiterated week after week through worship, engaging activities, and age-appropriate Bible teaching.

K—5th Grade

elementary
Your elementary kiddos will learn that life with Jesus is fun! In large group, the kids participate in a group game, dynamic worship, and an engaging teaching. In small groups, the kids will apply what they’ve learned through intentional discussion and application activities. Your kids will also leave with a JAM Time Sheet (“Jesus and Me”) that will help them grow in their relationship with Jesus during the week.

Special Needs

blossom

Families with members experiencing special needs can often feel as if they are alone and without support. We are dedicated to providing a loving, supportive environment where families and their kiddos with special needs can find sanctuary through which to grow and Blossom in their personal relationship with Jesus. Blossom programming is offered at all of our morning services.

If you plan to join us for the first time this Sunday, fill out the form below to let us know you're coming so we can prepare the safest environment for your kiddo!

What to Expect

new here? want to know more?

On your first visit, plan to arrive about 10-15 minutes before the start of the service, and let our team members know you’re visiting when you arrive. They’ll walk you directly to our Kids Desk where you’ll be registered and receive your child’s parent identification number.

In order to expedite the check-in process, you can pre-register your kiddo at the link below.

>> Register Now

Every child’s name tag is matched to a parent-identification number, every team member is screened with an interview and federal background check, and every hallway door is locked. During check out, you will show this security tag to your child’s small group leader in order for your child to be released from the classroom.

Every Sunday, you'll see our team members arriving in their orange shirts, ready to enthusiastically lead our kids in worship, facilitate small group activities, host first-time guest families, run production in large group, and so much more! Come join the fun!
