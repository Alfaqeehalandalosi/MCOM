Our Leadership

meet the team

Van Vandegriff

Senior Pastor

Van Vandegriff moved to Georgia from Waco, Texas, to pastor Cedarcrest Church in December of 2017. He graduated from Baylor University (BBA) and George W. Truett Theological Seminary (M.Div.), whereafter he and his wife, Kelly, became church planters in Berlin, Germany, for 8 years.

Pastor Van is passionate about Jesus, the local church, and global missions. When he isn't spearheading a new venture to see the local church built, he enjoys hunting, traveling, and spending time with his three teenagers and wife, Kelly.
