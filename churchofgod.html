Vision & Values

dedicating your heart to real life-change.

The Manifestation Discipleship School will help you become a disciple-maker by deepening your knowledge of the Bible, helping you apply its truths, and growing your leadership. We believe that the Discipleship School will equip you for success wherever God calls you; whether in family life, church planting, entrepreneurship, the marketplace, the arts, and beyond. Discipleship transforms us for a lifetime, not just a moment.

There will be no Manifestation Discipleship School in 2025, we are excited for what God has in store for MDS in the future!

What to Expect

relevant classes and real-life application for serious growth.

The Manifestation Discipleship School is a semester long school that offers you an environment where you will be trained, stretched, and transformed through encountering Jesus. You will discover the unique purpose and gifting God has put on your life to make a Kingdom impact. Throughout the year, you will be discipled, learn how to make disciples, and learn how to live on mission wherever God has you—in this city, our nation, or across the world.

the classroom

Class nights at The Discipleship School look like prayer, worship, teaching, and life-on-life discipleship that will help you not only discover your God-given purpose but also how to live it out. Tuesday's 6:30 - 9:30 pm.

Real-Life Application

You will read faith-building books, learn how to share your faith, develop intimacy with Jesus, practice the gifts of the spirit, and participate in an overseas mission trip.

A Transformed Life

Grow in your understanding of Scripture, who God is and His mission for His people. Our goal is that you would learn and be challenged to live a life on mission with Jesus through our various activities, teachings and speakers.

ready for the adventure of a lifetime? apply today!

  • Completion of Growth Track
  • Completion of a Freedom Small Group
  • Currently serving on a Serve Team
  • Have attended Manifestation for at least a year
